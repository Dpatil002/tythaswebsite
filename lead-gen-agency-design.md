INTERACTIONS AND ANIMATIONS

Use subtle, premium animations:

- Hero content fade and slide upward

- Dashboard graph draws on page load

- Metrics count upward when visible

- Cards rise by 4px on hover

- Timeline line animates while scrolling

- Buttons have subtle arrow movement

- Section content fades in sequentially

- Avoid excessive parallax and distracting motion

- Respect reduced-motion accessibility settings

RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOUR

Desktop:

- Use wide layouts and horizontal timelines

- Maintain clear content hierarchy

Tablet:

- Convert large sections into two-column layouts

- Reduce heading sizes and spacing

Mobile:

- Stack all sections vertically

- Keep CTA buttons full-width where appropriate

- Convert timelines into vertical steps

- Use horizontally scrollable case study and industry cards only when necessary

- Keep font sizes readable

- Minimum tap target: 44px

- Avoid oversized dashboard elements

- Keep form completion simple

ACCESSIBILITY

- Use strong colour contrast

- Add visible focus states

- Provide labels for every form field

- Add alt-text placeholders for images

- Do not communicate meaning using colour alone

- Use semantic heading hierarchy

- Avoid body text below 16px

COPYWRITING RULES

- Focus on outcomes, not generic claims

- Avoid words such as “revolutionary”, “world-class” and “best”

- Use clear and confident language

- Keep paragraphs short

- Use measurable results only when verified

- Every section should lead naturally toward a conversion action
